\begin{enumerate}
    \item Euclidean Algorithm. 
    \item Fast Exponentiation (using Fermat's Little Theorem). 
    \item Calculating $\phi(m)$ (get the prime factorization of $m$ then write $\phi(m) = m(1-\frac{1}{p_1})\dots(1-\frac{1}{p_n})$ where $m$ has $n$ distinct prime factors)
    \item Chinese Remainder Theorem. 
    \item RSA Cryptography (using the Lemma). 
    \item Hensel's Lemma. 
    \item Finding a Primitive root modulo $m$. (if $m$ is prime, then test the positive divisors of $m-1$)
    \item Taking Discrete Logarithm (if need to solve $x \equiv 1 \Mod{m}$, then we obtains the following solutions $x \equiv g^a \Mod{m}$ where $a \equiv 0, \frac{\phi(m)}{d}, \frac{2\phi(m)}{d}, \dots, \frac{(d-1)\phi(m)}{d} \Mod{\phi(m)}$)
    \item Finding number of primitive roots (if $m$ has a primitive root, there are $\phi(\phi(m))$ of them)
    \item Diffie-Hellman Key Exchange. 
    \item Solving Quadratic Congruences modulo $p$ (make leading coefficient of the polynomial $f(x)$ 1, then complete the square)
    \item Manipulation with Legendre/Jacobi symbols. 
    \item Reducing Binary Quadratic Forms. 
    \item Writing Finite Simple Continued Fractions (use Euclidean Algorithm). 
    \item Writing Infinite Simple Continued Fractions (find the part where it repeats, and assign it to be $\theta$ and solve the recurrence equation). 
\end{enumerate}
